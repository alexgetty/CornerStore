# Product/Bundle Detail Pages

## Context

Currently the storefront renders a single grid of cards. Detail pages would give each product or bundle its own page with richer content — per-item price breakdown for bundles, multiple images, extended descriptions.

## Considerations

- Single products vs bundles will differentiate more at the detail level
- Per-item price breakdown for bundles belongs here, not on the card
- Multi-image support (already noted as future work) ties into this

## Status

Future work.
